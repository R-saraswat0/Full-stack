Ash Ketchum's Legendary Journey

Ash traveled across all regions with Pikachu, becoming World Champion after defeating Leon in Galar!

  1. Kanto – Began journey, Top 16 at Indigo League
  2. Johto – Silver Conference, Top 8
  3. Hoenn – Top 8, met May and Max
  4. Sinnoh – Defeated Paul, Top 4
  5. Unova – New adventures, Top 8
  6. Kalos – Runner-up, Ash-Greninja bond
  7. Alola – Became Champion
  8. Galar – Crowned World Champion
  9. Paldea – New era with Liko & Roy
